Policies and procedures are part of a company's corporate knowledge which embody their approach to compliance with relevant regulation.

Policies need to address the company's stance on areas of risk, regulation and law. Procedures and processes need to translate these high level policies into more specific responsibilities and operating instructions.

Building a model of regulation and law using AI and vector databases allows Regulation Arcana to deploy a re-usable analysis for specific industry requirements.

Applying a Work Health and Safety model to a company's relevant policies and procedures allows alignments and mis-alignments to be surfaced for human interpretation.

Specific requirements in operating procedures, are linked with company policy and the company's regulatory and legal risks. Staff with this connected view are better able to understand and suggest improvements to policy and procedures.
