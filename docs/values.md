I value safety, dignity and rapport above all else.  
See [Today's ABA by Dr. Greg Hanley](https://practicalfunctionalassessment.com/2021/09/09/a-perspective-on-todays-aba-by-dr-greg-hanley/)

- **Compassion**: Fostering an encouraging environment where individuals feel safe and supported.
- **Growth**: Helping children develop new skills and overcome challenges.
- **Integrity**: Maintaining honesty and transparency in all interactions.
- **Engagement**: Interactive and fun approach to enhance learning.
- **Collaboration**: Working closely with families, caregivers, and communities.
- **Inclusivity**: Celebrating diversity and tailoring support to each child.
- **Empowerment**: Equipping individuals with tools and confidence to thrive.
